Speeding

So this week I got a speeding ticket, this was a real wake up call for me and also scaring the ever living shit out of me at the same time.

Due to the aspects of my job I need to be driving and have a valid driving license. This recent speeding ticket to me was a huge wake up call for my driving. As I was going to Aintree for the Grand National I got caught going 58 in a 50 zone and this has really shook me up and made me think about how I need to be driving.

After this ticket I am now going to be taking extra care on how I drive and making sure I follow the limit.

Although not many things have happened since my last post with the exception of the speeding ticket there have still been some developments that might be worth updating you about.
While I am becoming more and more interested in making this a blog website more than anything I still want to focus on some VFX work. I have recently been watching Corridor Digital on YouTube and it has really made me want to have a house which me and Abii can make our own. I really want to be able to have our own a place and have access to the funds to create a space that is contemporary and with a stylish-esque sort of setting for me to relax.

Hopefully sometime this year I would like to move from our current flat to a much bigger house where we have our own kitchen area and then a living room. I would like to have a nice place which we could make our own and have a drive and this would be a place I would like to stay and be there so we can start to have a family and have a place that is more comfortable for us.

While this has been a short blog this week, I hope you have enjoyed this short read. While I try to think of something to type each week that is interesting I have become stuck with trying to find something to talk about as my life really isn't that interesting to talk about. However I will keep trying to find something to talk about on this blog.

Thanks,
